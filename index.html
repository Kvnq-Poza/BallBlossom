Partners

Our Partners

Irish Mirror

The Irish Mirror is a national daily tabloid newspaper published in Ireland by Reach plc. It is the sister paper of the Daily Mirror, which is published in England, Wales, and Scotland. The Irish Mirror was founded in 1900 as the Irish Daily Mirror, and it has been published under its current name since 1993.

Irish Football Association

The Irish Football Association (IFA) is the governing body of association football in Northern Ireland. It was founded in 1880, and it is one of the four home nations of the International Football Association Board (IFAB). The IFA is responsible for organizing and running the Northern Ireland national football team.

Goal360

Goal360 is a sports news website that provides news, analysis, and opinion on a wide range of sports, including association football, rugby union, Gaelic games, and golf. The website was founded in 2012, and it is one of the most popular sports news websites in Ireland.
